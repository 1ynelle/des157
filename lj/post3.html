Gucci Gift-Giving Site

Gucci has a dedicated website for their 2020 gift-giving campaign which incorporates images of their gift collections in a retro office party environment. The site uses very subtle party decorations and colors, and it was interesting to see how Gucci products can fit in into a mundane, everyday environment.

The visitor can click to zoom in to an area of the room and click on one of the hotspots which gives the name, image of the product, and a link to shop the collection. Having their collection laid out like this makes it simple to find a product and offers a more immersive experience than a long list to choose from on a shopping website. The subdued music and room moving in relation to the cursor also adds to the 3-dimensionality, and overall makes the site fun to explore!
