Form Design Best Practices

After reading Salim Ansari's article, "Best Practices for Form Design", I learned that the goal is to help the user fill out and submit the entire form. This means making requirements easy to see and mistakes simple to fix.

Tips that stood out to me were adding supporting text and identification (in addition to color) to improve visual accessibility. Other points were helpful in reducing frustrations I had when filling out forms such as having a show password option and being sparing with autofill information. Some tips I think I'll incorporate when designing my Mad Libs form are one column layouts and making form fields a similar length to the input.

While certain points seemed obvious at first, I realized many were easy to overlook when I'm not the one having trouble completing a form.
