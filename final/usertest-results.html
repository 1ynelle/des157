User Test Results

One of the main issues my classmates had while testing my site was not knowing the images on each page were clickable. They thought the drop shadow was a nice indicator, but making it darker or adding a transition where the images scale larger would make the interaction more intuitive.

The map graphic and font helped to make the site feel personal, although adding some color to the right would help make the pages less left-focused. The navigation is intuitive, but there can be more indication of where the next and previous links lead to.

Other suggestions for making my site more cohesive included changing the background color scheme, adding a map or subtle background on the home page, and adding a crossfading effect to the home page slideshow.

I would probably also fill in the placeholder text and add points on the map graphic to show where each place is located.
